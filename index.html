Membership Policy

At Cannapure, we value our community of members. To maintain a secure and exclusive environment, purchases are restricted to members only. Please ensure your membership is current before visiting us.

Product Availability

In-Store Purchases Only: We invite you to visit our store to explore our products firsthand. Currently, we do not offer online sales or shipping services. This ensures that you receive personalized service and product guidance.

Disclaimer

Cannapure is committed to responsible retailing. We strictly enforce legal age restrictions. You must be of legal age to enter our premises and make purchases.

Non-members and individuals under the legal age are not permitted to purchase products. We reserve the right to verify age and membership status at any time.
